Engineered for the Cloud

In addition to physical infrastructure, CloudRouter® is also engineered to run on private and public cloud platforms and can be installed at scale with a fully-automated configuration system.

Minimal Resource Consumption

The CloudRouter® image is smaller, so you can deploy faster. The initial community release of CloudRouter® is under 1GB, including a full OpenDaylight distribution.

Container-Ready

Future releases of CloudRouter® will include images for multiple container technologies. Whether you’re rolling out a handful of containers, or scaling up to meet incredible demand, you’ll be able to do it with CloudRouter®.

Secure Connectivity

Connect securely to your CloudRouter® via standards-based IPSec VPN, or provide secure network access to remote users via SSL-based OpenVPN or L2TP over IPSec VPN functionality.

Monitoring and Reporting

Logging and diagnostic information at your finger tips via SNMP, Netflow, and Syslog.  Integrated network protocol analyzer lets you see what’s happening on your network at a microscopic level.

High Availability

Deploy CloudRouter® with confidence knowing that high availability and system redundancy can be achieved through standard failover and synchronization mechanisms.
